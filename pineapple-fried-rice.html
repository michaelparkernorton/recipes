Pineapple Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • Cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • Vegetables (such as diced carrots, peas, bell peppers, onions)
  • Protein (such as diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu)
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Oil (such as vegetable or sesame oil)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add a bit of oil.
  2. Add minced garlic to the hot oil and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add your choice of vegetables and protein to the skillet/wok and cook until they are tender and cooked through.
  4. Push the cooked vegetables and protein to one side of the skillet/wok and crack the eggs into the empty side.
  5. Scramble the eggs until they are cooked through, then mix them with the vegetables and protein.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the skillet/wok and stir everything together until well combined.
  7. Drizzle soy sauce over the rice mixture and continue to stir-fry for a few more minutes until everything is heated through and well coated with the soy sauce.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired, and serve hot.
