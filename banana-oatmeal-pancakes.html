Banana Oatmeal Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup groats
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: maple syrup, sliced bananas, berries for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the ripe bananas, groats, milk, eggs, baking powder, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Blend the mixture until smooth. If the batter is too thick, you can add a little more milk to thin it out.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with cooking spray or a bit of oil.
  4. Once the skillet is hot, pour about 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  5. Cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes on one side, or until bubbles form on the surface and the edges start to look set.
  6. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side, until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter, adding more oil to the skillet as needed.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with your choice of toppings, such as maple syrup, sliced bananas, or berries.
