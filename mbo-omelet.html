MBO Omelet

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper (any color you prefer)
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • Optional: grated cheese, chopped herbs (like parsley or chives)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil or butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms and diced bell pepper to the skillet. Cook until they are tender, about 5-6 minutes.
  4. While the vegetables are cooking, crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork until well mixed. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Once the vegetables are tender, spread them out evenly in the skillet.
  6. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables, ensuring they cover the entire surface of the skillet.
  7. Allow the omelet to cook undisturbed for a few minutes, until the edges start to set.
  8. Gently lift the edges of the omelet with a spatula and tilt the skillet to let any uncooked egg flow to the edges.
  9. Once the omelet is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, sprinkle grated cheese over the vegetables if desired.
  10. Carefully fold one side of the omelet over the other using the spatula, creating a half-moon shape.
  11. Cook for another minute or until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked through.
  12. Slide the omelet onto a plate and garnish with chopped herbs if desired.
  13. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious mushroom, bell pepper, and onion omelet!
